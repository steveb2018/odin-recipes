Breakfast Cereal

Description

This is a recipe for breakfast cereal detailing the ingredients and the steps required to make it. Note: this only works with pre-made and pre-packaged cerals that do not need to be heated.

Ingredients

  • Breakfast cereal of your choice
  • Optional: Milk

Steps

  1. Retrieve the container of breakfast cereal.
  2. Optional: Retrieve milk.
  3. Pour the desired amount of breakfast cereal into a bowl.
  4. Optional: Pour the desired amount of milk over the breakfast ceral.
  5. Eat the cereal - hopefully I don't need to tell you how to eat.
