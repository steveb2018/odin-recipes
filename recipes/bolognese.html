Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

This is a recipe for spaghetti bolognese detailing the ingredients and the steps required to make it.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 (28 ounce) cans crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pan of lightly salted water to boil.
  2. Cook spaghetti in boiling water for 12 minutes.
  3. Stir the onion and oregano.
  4. Cook until vegetables begin to soften - around 8 minutes.
  5. Add garlic to the mix and cook until fragrant - around 2 minutes.
  6. Crumble the ground beef into the vegetable mixture.
  7. stir until the beef is completely cooked and no longer pink - around 8 to 10 minutes
  8. Stir the curshed tomatoes and tomato paste into the ground beef mixture.
  9. Bring ground beef mixture to a boil.
  10. Remove ground beef mixture from heat
  11. Ladle the sauce over the cooked spaghetti.
